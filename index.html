How it works?
This is a demonstration of a ordering system based on McDonald's which utilises face recognition software to improve your ordering experience.
Once you upload a photo of yourself, we get to work analysing that photo to determine your mood, age and gender. The outcome is a customised menu to help you decide on what to order.
